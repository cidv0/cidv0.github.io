Trait bundles that keep momentum

Traits cost or refund points, enabling unique utility that jobs cannot provide. Combine high-impact positives with manageable negatives; swap in equivalents that fit your server settings or comfort level.

Solo stealth package

  • Fanny Pack (10 pts): +2 hotbar slots; offsets limited ammo/utility space.
  • Inconspicuous: Lowers enemy detection radius while crouched.
  • Night Owl: Stamina regen buff in darkness.
  • Negatives: Weak Bladder & Dry Skin add mild upkeep without touching combat stats.

Pairs naturally with Lab Assistant or Summer Intern builds focused on terminals and loot runs.

Co-op defender toolkit

  • Self Defense: Boosts Blunt & Sharp damage; helps while waiting for firearms.
  • Weathered: Adds environmental resistances, great for storm runs.
  • Resistances (if unlocked): Extra hazard mitigation without needing full suits.
  • Negatives: Hearty Appetite & Narcoleptic keep trait points flowing but are co-op friendly.

Recommended for Trans-Kinematic Researcher or Defense Analyst when a squad can cover food and rest cycles.

Utility scientist bundle

  • Wrinkly Brainmeat: +15% XP lets support roles keep pace with fighters.
  • Gray Matter: Extra research slots for puzzle terminals.
  • Fanny Pack or Organized: Carry more samples & kits.
  • Negatives: Slow Healer & Weak Bladder are tolerable with stocked med bays.

Great with Bionomicist or Paratheoretical Physicist for teams that rotate jobs or run research expeditions.

Trait availability shifts as the game updates. Keep the planner synced with the in-game creator so the point costs and unlock tags stay accurate.

Role archetypes & coordination tips

Jobs only dictate your opening moves — long-term success comes from matching your specialization with the team’s needs and the expedition plan.

Solo explorers

  • Summer Intern offers the largest trait sandbox; funnel points into mobility, storage, and survivability.
  • Lab Assistant excels at dungeon crawling once stealth perks stack.
  • Carry multi-role tools: taser, pry-bar, and a portable light to offset trait negatives like Night Owl.

Co-op splits

  • Bionomicist + Gastrologist: share cook/medic duties while the rest push sectors.
  • Archotechnic Consultant should stay near base to unlock tier 2 benches quickly.
  • Defense Analyst needs consistent XP feeders (range practice, turret placements) to offset Slower Learner.

Log trait experiments in the planner so the team knows which builds are proven on your server version.
