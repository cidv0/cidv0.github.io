Abiotic Factor - Day 1 & Night 1 Quick Survival

One-page, screenshot-ready infographic - Focus: character setup, first base, night run, must-have gear.

Day 06:00 Night 21:00 Power Off at Night

Character Setup

  • Fanny Pack trait -> +2 hotbar slots (unique). Costs 10 points.
  • Summer Intern job grants 12 points -> easy cover for the trait.
  • Pick what fits you, just skip the tough traits on a first run.

Starting Area (No Time Pressure)

  • Time does not start until you leave. Loot at your pace.
  • Bathrooms: relieve and wash; sinks provide clean water.
  • Wear the Chef's Hat (free slot). Grab the Medkit and Flashlight.
  • Email Terminals reveal recipes. Read them before heading out.
  • Grab everything. Drop extras on the floor; they are safe short-term.

Food & Cooking

  • First knife and stove live in the cafeteria kitchen.
  • Butcher enemies -> raw parts -> fry in a pan for safe food.
  • No pan? Craft a makeshift version at the bench.

First Bench = First Safety

Crafting Bench (keep duct tape + metal pipes) reduces nearby enemy spawns. Build it as soon as you step out.

Bench spot we like: Office sector -> kitchenette -> locker -> gym -> upstairs room.

Backpacks

  • Backpack by the NPC desk (next room) -> +1 inventory row.
  • Security Pack (better) -> office balcony ladder -> down to the body -> +3 slots.

Base Essentials to Grab or Build

  • Storage Crates (smash wood crates for planks).
  • Standing Lamps provide light without power.
  • Water Coolers keep thirst topped off.
  • Makeshift Bed -> pack couches (50% success) to unlock the recipe.
  • Medkit Wall Box (unscrew) for storage and bench upgrades.
  • Smash PCs in cubicles for screws and electronics.
  • Grab a Soil Bag nearby for future planting.

Power & Utilities

  • Battery -> socket -> battery -> device for overnight power.
  • Plug Strip extends outlets to multiple devices.
  • Portable Stove lets you cook anywhere.
  • Repair & Salvage Station fixes gear; needs an Anvil (see Night Run).

Night Run: Early Anvil

Begins 21:00 - power off, colder, security robot patrols.

  • Avoid fighting the robot. Not worth it early.
  • Route: back to cafeteria -> right vent -> forward vent (fan is off at night).
  • Dismantle metal scrap -> clear peckery -> through blue doors -> grab Anvil, tomato seeds, 9mm ammo.
  • Dash back to base. Night is short (about a third of the day).

Bench Upgrades (Soon)

  • Auto-Heal heals while you stand nearby.
  • Item Transporter pulls materials from nearby crates.
  • Bench Warmer grants passive heat while crafting.

Night Encounters

  • Coworker (creepy crawler) is a friendly trader. Feed food to earn staplers and pens.
  • Security Sector: skip for now.

Pro Tips

  • Keep duct tape and metal pipes on you for emergency bench placement.
  • Label crates with categories to speed up crafting later.
  • Carry a flashlight (fresh batteries) before dusk.
  • Loot every box of screws you see. High value.
  • Do not fear death; you can recover your death bag.
  • Explore the facility for shortcuts, seeds, and side-grades.
