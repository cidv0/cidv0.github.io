Combat Prep & Resistances

Armor & Gear

Protective sets keep researchers alive long enough to finish the job. This field guide highlights the armor families, environmental suits, and support gadgets worth packing, plus how to maintain them without burning through scarce repair kits.1

Field reminder

Keep one full repair kit and spare arm guards on a wall hook. Light pieces break first, and staging replacements saves a bench trip mid-raid.1,2

Quick answer

Mix ballistic sets with hazard suits so the squad can swap roles on demand, track encumbrance to keep stamina healthy, and repair gear at the Workbench before durability falls below half to avoid stat penalties.1,3

Security & ballistic sets

  • Security, Jugaar, and F.O.R.G.E. pieces layer armor rating without sending encumbrance into the red.1
  • Pair heavy chestplates with lighter greaves so stamina drain stays manageable during firefights.
  • Keep riot or hardlight shields packed near doors for breach defense and choke point pushes.

Environmental suits

  • Hazardous Materials, Fire Proximity, and Rat suits fully replace chest and leg slots for specific hazard runs.1
  • Excursion and Diving suits trade armor for exploration perks like oxygen and hazard immunity—deploy only when needed.
  • Stage a clean locker for each suit so contamination does not spread back into the safe house.

Specialist accessories

  • Head slot upgrades like GATE NVGs or Maestro Casque define visibility and crit perks for the squad spotter.1
  • Backpacks such as Security Pack, Research Pack, or Jetpack set how far you can run loot without slowing.
  • Trinkets (Geiger Counter Keychain, Dangling Gravity Cube) add utility without weight—rotate them between players.

Slot priorities & swapping logic

Track each equipment slot so you know which pieces must stay pristine and which can be swapped for hazard coverage.

Head & vision

  • Keep a Hard Hat or Security Helmet near engineering tasks to negate falling debris.
  • NVG variants grant night sight but increase repair costs—pack spares rather than risking a break mid-mission.

Arms & utility

  • Containment Gloves and F.O.R.G.E. Pauldrons push resistances while keeping encumbrance low.
  • Repair sleeves first—broken arm guards block passives like recoil control or crafting speed buffs.

Chest cores

  • Security and Jugaar chestplates are reliable defaults; swap to Ionized Coreplate for electrical storms.
  • Full suits override chest stats—stash your ballistic plate so it is ready when the suit comes off.

Leg mobility

  • Mountaineer or Cascade legplates boost stamina regen in long corridors; heavy sabatons slow sprint starts.
  • Monitor encumbrance each time you swap boots—overweight penalties hit dodge rolls hard.

Backpack slot

  • Security Pack balances capacity and weight for daily ops; Research Pack keeps samples cold for delivery.
  • Jetpack and Voidpack consume power—store charged batteries near the deploy point.

Trinkets & gadgets

  • Assign Geiger Counter Keychain to the scout; rotate Hardlight Shield Generator for boss phases.
  • Store trinkets in labeled crates to highlight their passive effects for newer teammates.

Mixing sets without losing bonuses

Full armor sets grant passive perks, but smart swapping keeps those perks active while covering hazards.

Anchor one core set

Maintain a primary ballistic kit per player (Security, Jugaar, F.O.R.G.E.) and only swap individual slots when a hazard demands it. The remaining pieces preserve set bonuses like recoil control or crafting speed.1

Treat suits as mission items

Full body suits should be staged like consumables. Equip them for radiation, biohazard, or fire runs, then immediately switch back to the anchored set so you regain passive combat perks.

Coordinate accessory swaps

Share high-impact trinkets and backpacks based on the mission roster. Hand the Dangling Gravity Cube to the hauler, give NVGs to the scout, and pass the Riot Shield to the leading breacher.

Maintenance & upgrade loop

Repairs and upgrades draw from multiple benches—stage materials and track durability before it reaches critical.

1. Inspect & triage

  • Check durability after every raid; under 50% risks stat loss or breakage.
  • Sort broken pieces by slot so repair mats can be queued efficiently.

2. Prep materials

  • Workbenches handle early repairs; advanced armor may need Chemistry or Electronics benches for reagents.3
  • Keep adhesives, plating, and spare wiring in labeled crates next to the benches.

3. Upgrade path

  • Unlock higher-tier armor through faction drops or Fabricator schematics as the campaign progresses.1
  • Use the Packaging Station to duplicate signage and storage that explain each set bonus.

4. Rotation & storage

  • Assign lockers per player plus one shared hazard locker for suits and trinkets.
  • Log set ownership on a whiteboard so items do not vanish into personal inventories.

Sample loadouts to copy

Security breach team

  • Hardlight Shield, Military Pack, Dangling Gravity Cube.
  • NVGs for point, Maestro Casque for overwatch sniper.

Surface expedition

  • Excursion Suit with Mountaineer boots for stamina and hazard cover.
  • Jetpack or Voidpack with Battery Wall charging station ready at base.
  • Carry a spare Security chestplate to swap back once hazards clear.
