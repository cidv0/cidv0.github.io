Base Infrastructure

Crafting Stations

Crafting stations turn raw scrap and ideas into usable gear, food, and upgrades while projecting safety perks around your base. This guide tracks every workstation you can build, the resources they consume, how to keep them powered through nightly blackouts, and the recommended build order for solo and co-op crews.

Field reminder

Every powered bench doubles as a spawn suppressor. Wire your main bench into a dedicated battery circuit before nightfall to prevent portal storms from spawning inside your lab.1

Quick answer

Rush the Crafting Bench first, keep it powered with at least a makeshift battery, and expand into salvage, cooking, and enhancement once you can spare coils and refined parts.

Bench priority

  • Crafting Bench blueprint unlocks during the prologue; build it before night one ends.1
  • Repair & Salvage Station is the next milestone so you can recycle broken gear into scrap.2
  • Enhancement Bench enters the loop once you have steady Exor drops and weapon cores.3

Power discipline

  • Makeshift batteries (50 charge) cover one bench for 250 seconds; chain two to span a full night.8
  • Industrial batteries (100 charge) power two devices but still dip for 20 seconds at dawn.8
  • Install a Charging Station near the hub so energy tools and tasers stay mission-ready.7

Co-op workflow

  • Only one player can queue crafts per bench; others can access finished output immediately.
  • Item Transporter upgrades pull from shared lockers within 16 m, reducing inventory juggling.1
  • Label station circuits so teammates know which breaker or battery to swap at sunset.

Crafting Bench

Core hub

Default workstation for nearly every idea. Establishes your safe zone, anchors teleporters, and unlocks tiered upgrades that supercharge base logistics.1

Unlock & recipe

  • Unlock: Tutorial reward; rebuild after the prologue evac.
  • Crafting Bench ×1
    • Duct Tape ×4
    • Power Supply Unit ×1
    • Metal or Hydro Pipe ×2

Power & upkeep

  • Requires continuous power for recipe access and spawn suppression. Tie it to batteries before lights-out.
  • Portal Suppression upgrade destroys nearby Power Leeches during Rolling Blackouts if the bench stays live.
  • Package with any construction tool to relocate; redeployment desyncs teleporters until you rescan them.1,8

Upgrades worth rushing

  • Item Transporter (Crafting 2)—auto pulls from lockers within 16 m.1
  • Bench Warmer (Crafting 2)—stops Chilly debuff; toggle via exposed coils.1
  • Portal Suppression (Crafting 5)—nullifies portal storms near your main base.1
  • Spontaneous Matter Synthesizer (Crafting 15)—generates random scrap while idle.1

Repair & Salvage Station

Maintenance

Repairs armor, weapons, and tools while letting you scrap dupes back into core materials without losing value.2

Unlock & recipe

  • Unlock: Construction idea drops in Office Sector tool rooms.
  • Repair & Salvage Station ×1
    • Duct Tape ×6
    • Coil ×2
    • Anvil ×1
    • Metal or Hydro Pipe ×2

Power & usage

  • Shares the Crafting Bench safe-zone radius when powered, giving you overlapping spawn suppression.2
  • Recommended to wire into the same battery circuit as your bench so Item Transporter pulls repair mats automatically.
  • Salvaging here grants crafting XP, unlike the Auto-Salvager.

Co-op tip: Stage bulk repair materials (duct tape, metal scrap, weapon cores) in the same locker the Item Transporter can see. Players can queue repairs back to back without shuffling inventory.

Chef’s Counter

Nutrition

Dual-sided cooking station for high-tier food recipes and fish butchering. Essential once hunger multipliers ramp up in multiplayer.4

Unlock & recipe

  • Unlock: Culinary idea nodes in the Office Sector cafeteria.
  • Chef’s Counter ×1
    • Wood Plank ×8
    • Power Supply Unit ×2
    • Metal or Hydro Pipe ×4
    • Metal Scrap ×4

Power & upgrades

  • Must stay powered to access cooking and fish prep tabs. Place near your pantry to leverage Item Transporter.4
  • Item Transporter—identical to the bench upgrade, requires Crafting 2 and pulls from nearby fridges.4
  • Cheffigy—summons Dr. Carson for rare ingredient trades after repeat encounters.4

Recommend pairing with Refrigerators and a Hand Sink to keep food buffs rolling. Label ingredient lockers so co-op cooks can prep bundles quickly.

Auto-Salvager & support gear

Automation

Hands-off salvage converts raid junk while Charging Stations keep energy weapons topped off between sorties.5,7

Auto-Salvager

  • Unlock: Advanced Engineering idea nodes (late Office Sector / early Cascade).
  • Recipe
    • Air Compressor ×1
    • Chain ×4
    • Magnetic Alloy ×4
    • Energy Brick ×2
  • Requires power; does not generate crafting XP, so rotate between manual salvage for leveling.5

Charging Station

  • Unlock: Found as loot or crafted once you find a Car Battery Charger.
  • Recipe
    • Car Battery Charger ×1
    • Coil ×4
    • Metal Pipe ×4
  • Plug into wall outlets during the day; at night feed it from your bench circuit to top off tasers, flashlights, and hacking tools.7

Drop Auto-Salvagers on rolling carts or beside loot sorting tables so teammates can dump haul bags, flip the breaker, and walk away while the machine churns.

Power continuity & outages

Nights last 270 seconds. Plan battery coverage so benches stay online and Item Transporter upgrades keep pulling stock.

Battery math

  • Makeshift (50 charge) → 250 s for one device.
  • Industrial (100 charge) → 250 s for two devices (expect 20 s outage).
  • Chain batteries only when you must; upstream cells drain faster per device.*8

Outage effects

  • Bench safe zones collapse immediately, allowing portal storms or assaults to spawn inside.1,8
  • Locked recipes gray out until power returns; queued crafts pause, not cancel.
  • Teleport sync points remain valid even if the bench is offline.

Automation tips

  • Mount breakers and labels above each outlet so co-op squads know which battery bank to swap.
  • Schedule a nightfall checklist: replace drained cells, empty Auto-Salvager bins, top off Charging Station.
  • Consider day/night speed tweak (1.1) if you want perfect battery coverage without micro-managing.8

*Upstream batteries count downstream batteries as devices; place critical benches closest to the power source.

Co-op etiquette & relocation

Smooth bench rotations keep your lab efficient even with six scientists crafting at once.

Queue management

  • Stations are exclusive-use; voice who is crafting and drop materials into shared lockers.
  • Use benches’ output queue as a staging shelf; anyone can loot finished crafts even if they did not start them.
  • Auto-Salvager avoids UI contention—dump loot in bulk while another player uses the manual station.

Moving stations

  • Equip any construction tool (Screwdriver or better), target the bench, and choose Package to recover it intact.6
  • Packing player-placed items never fails; world props may break if your Construction skill is low.6
  • Reinstall on a ghost hologram, rotate for cable runs, and reconnect to your battery strip.
