Water Sources

  • Sinks and Water Coolers give clean water early. Unscrew and relocate coolers to your base.
  • Tainted Sources (fountains, leaks, pools) are plentiful but unsafe until purified.
  • Carry Containers like pots, bottles, and barrels to haul and stockpile wherever you settle.
Stage a "water corner" at base: cooler for dispensing, barrel for bulk, and a labeled crate for empty bottles.

Purification Methods

  • Boil It - cooking pot plus any stove converts tainted -> clean. Run multiple pots in parallel.
  • Filter It - late-game Water Filter on a barrel converts tainted -> clean efficiently.
  • Stay Safe - drinking unpurified water causes sickness. Keep basic meds handy when testing.
Upgrade path: portable stove -> multiple pots -> water filter attached to a stationary barrel.

Storage and Scaling

  • Water Coolers are great for quick dispensing near crafting benches.
  • Barrels act as high-capacity reservoirs; pair them with filters.
  • Label Containers for "Clean (Drink)", "Tainted (Process)", and "Empty Bottles".
Position barrels under steady drips or leaks to collect tainted water passively.

"Infinite" Water Loop

Base near a tainted source -> capture -> purify -> store.

  1. Claim a Source: pick a reliable fountain, pool, or leak near your base route.
  2. Capture: fill pots or bottles, or park a barrel to collect tainted water.
  3. Purify: boil early on; swap to a water filter on the barrel once unlocked.
  4. Distribute: decant into coolers and bottles labeled "Clean". Keep a spare cooler at secondary hubs.
Avoid long trips with heavy liquids at night. Stage mini-stations along safe corridors.

Medicine & Healing

  • Scavenge First Aid in wall boxes, clinics, and med rooms. Unscrew wall kits for storage and crafting.
  • Tier Your Usage - use basic bandages or food for chip damage; save medkits for emergencies.
  • Stock Reagents like antiseptics, cloth, and chemicals to unlock stronger items as benches advance.
Keep a "triage crate" by your bed or bench with bandages, antiseptics, and a spare flashlight.

Long-Term Resource Flow

  • Farm Once Stable - after solving water, plant seeds near light and heat sources.
  • Electronics & Metal - smash PCs and cubicles for screws and circuits; hoard pipes and duct tape.
  • Respawn Loops - revisit side zones or portals regularly for refreshed materials.
Place benches near loot-dense offices; upgrades like Item Transporter speed crafting loops.

Quick Checklist

  • Park a barrel under a steady leak or near a fountain.
  • Run two cooking pots and any stove for early purification.
  • Relocate a cooler to your base for clean refills.
  • Label crates for Clean, Tainted, and Empty bottles.
  • Unscrew a med wall box for storage plus a crafting component.
  • Stock screws, tape, and pipes; keep spare tool durability.
  • Stage safe corridor caches with lighting and a small water supply.
  • Target a water filter as your mid or late-game upgrade.

Timing Tips

Do heavy hauling in daylight. Night: avoid long water trips, process at base. Restock meds before big excursions.
