Guiding Lands Guide

Everything you need to know about the Guiding Lands in Monster Hunter World: Iceborne.

# Topic Description
1 What are the Guiding Lands? The Guiding Lands is an endgame mode with unique monsters, materials, and regions to explore. Unlocks after completing the main story.
2 Regions There are four regions: Ancient Forest, Coral Highlands, Rotten Vale, and Wildspire Waste. Each can be leveled from 1 to 7.
3 Leveling Regions Level up regions by fighting monsters and gathering tracks. Leveling one region may lower others. Focus on two regions at a time for efficiency.
4 Level Caps Level caps increase at Master Ranks 49, 69, and 99. Complete special quests from the Field Team Leader to raise these caps.
5 Efficient Master Rank Grinding Stay in the Guiding Lands for fast MR levelingâ€”no loading screens and continuous monster spawns. Investigations are good for rewards but slower.
6 Gathering Points Exclusive ores and bones can be found in the Guiding Lands. Look for "Big Gathering Points" for rare materials. These do not show on the map.
7 Exclusive Monsters
  • Scarred Yian Garuga (Ancient Forest, Level 6, MR 69)
  • Brute Tigrex (Rotten Vale, Level 6, MR 69)
  • Gold Rathian (Wildspire Waste, Level 6, MR 69)
  • Silver Rathalos (Coral Highlands, Level 6, MR 69)
  • Zinogre and Yian Garuga unlock earlier at lower levels.
8 Special Tracks Analyze special tracks to lure monsters. Fill the analysis bar by fighting monsters, then report to the Handler to lure out specific monsters.
9 Track Gathering Look for large tracks labeled as "Signs of Turf War" to quickly gain analyzed special tracks.
10 Tips for Teams Join communities like Discord for finding reliable groups. Coordinated teams make leveling regions and grinding much faster.

Final Tips

  • Focus on leveling specific regions for the monsters and materials you need.
  • Keep an eye on "Big Gathering Point" notifications for rare materials.
  • Maximize efficiency by combining Master Rank grinding and region leveling.
