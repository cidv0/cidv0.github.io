Alatreon Beginner's Guide

A quick and easy guide to help you defeat Alatreon in Monster Hunter World: Iceborne.

Understanding Alatreon

  • Elemental States:
    • Fire: Weak to Ice.
    • Ice: Weak to Fire.
    • Dragon: Focus on breaking horns.
  • Elemental Topple: Suppresses Eschaton Judgment. Deal elemental damage to trigger this.
  • Flying Phase: Use smoke bombs or wait for it to land naturally.

Key Skills

  • Blight Resistance (Level 2): Reduces blight durations.
  • Evade Window (Level 3): Easier dodging and higher DPS uptime.
  • Mindâ€™s Eye: Prevents bouncing on blue sharpness weapons.
  • Fortify: Boosts attack (+20%) and defense (+30%) after fainting.

Recommended Weapons

  • Elemental:
    • Kjarr Fire/Ice weapons (from Kulve Taroth).
    • Alternatives: Rathalos, Glavenus, or Barioth weapons.
  • Raw:
    • Great Sword, Hammer, or Pierce Bowguns.
    • Use Fortify to survive Eschaton Judgment.

Armor Recommendations

  • Safijiiva Armor: High DPS if available.
  • Mix high-defense parts with utility skills (Blight Resistance, Evade Window, etc.).

Combat Strategy

  • Preparation: Bring Astera Jerky, Sushifish Scales, Max Potions, and Health Boosters.
  • Focus Elemental Damage: Use Fire or Ice weapons based on the current state. Aim for the forelegs.
  • Dragon Phase: Break horns to prevent elemental state switching.

Final Tips

  • Practice to learn attack patterns.
  • Coordinate in multiplayer but stay flexible.
  • Survival > DPS when starting out!
