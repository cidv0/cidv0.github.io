Top 10 Mistakes Against Safi'jiiva

Improve your Safi'jiiva hunts by avoiding these common mistakes!

1. Treating it like a normal hunt

Safi'jiiva is like Kulve Taroth: the goal is to break parts, not just kill it. Focus on breaking all parts to maximize rewards.

2. Not using Partbreaker

Partbreaker is essential for Safi'jiiva hunts. If it’s not in your build, craft the charm or adjust your gear.

3. Rebreaking parts

Once a part is broken, stop attacking it! Focus on unbroken parts to conserve Safi'jiiva’s health for additional breaks.

4. Mounting too early

Save mounts for Stage 3 when Safi'jiiva enters its supercritical state. Use glider mantles or ledges to mount strategically.

5. Wasting status effects

Save sleep, paralysis, and KO for Stage 3. These control effects are much more effective during the supercritical state.

6. Misusing traps

Use environmental traps (explosions) wisely. Ensure Safi'jiiva is not in an animation or flinching to maximize their impact.

7. Using poison

Avoid poisoning Safi'jiiva as it drains its health without contributing to part breaks. Use Blast for better results.

8. Not healing teammates

Carry Dust of Life or Life Powders to heal teammates grabbed by Safi’jiiva. These items are free and critical for team success.

9. Ignoring team buffs

Use Demon Powder, Mega Demon Drugs, and Might Seeds to boost your team's attack power. Refresh buffs every 3 minutes.

10. Using the wrong weapon

The Aqua Shot Light Bowgun with water ammo is the best choice. Its fast, consistent damage works on all parts and excels in speedruns.

Weapon Recommendation

  • Aqua Shot Light Bowgun
  • Prioritize water elemental ammo for consistent part damage.

Final Tips

  • Check the part status in the menu to prioritize breaks.
  • Coordinate with teammates to maximize efficiency.
  • Practice makes perfect—refinement leads to better rewards!
