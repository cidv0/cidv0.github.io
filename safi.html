Top 10 Mistakes Against Safi'jiiva

Avoid these mistakes to improve your Safi'jiiva hunts and maximize rewards!

# Tip Description
1 Treating it like a normal hunt Safi'jiiva rewards are based on part breaks, not just defeating the monster. Break all parts before killing it for maximum rewards.
2 Not using Partbreaker Partbreaker is essential. If your build lacks it, craft the charm or adjust your gear.
3 Rebreaking parts Once a part is broken, stop attacking it. Focus on unbroken parts to conserve health for additional breaks.
4 Mounting too early Save mounts for Stage 3 when Safi'jiiva enters its supercritical state for maximum team damage.
5 Wasting status effects Save sleep, paralysis, and KO for Stage 3 to control Safi'jiiva during its supercritical state.
6 Misusing traps Use environmental traps effectively by ensuring Safi'jiiva is not in an animation or flinching before activation.
7 Using poison Avoid poison as it drains health without contributing to part breaks. Use Blast instead.
8 Not healing teammates Carry Dust of Life or Life Powders to save teammates grabbed by Safi'jiiva and for general team-wide healing.
9 Ignoring team buffs Use Demon Powder, Mega Demon Drugs, and Might Seeds to boost your team's attack power. Refresh every 3 minutes.
10 Using the wrong weapon The Aqua Shot Light Bowgun with water ammo is the best option for consistent damage across all parts.
